Welcome!

Welcome to my site! My name is Chandler Maggard. I am currently a teacher at Johnson Elementary about to begin my third year. I am working on becoming a school library media specialist. I am so excited to continue working with kids. I look forward to helping students find books they enjoy, helping teachers find resources, and teaching good research/digital citizenship skills.

Why I Wanted to Become a Librarian

From an early age, I loved stories. I loved listening to them before bed, hearing them from my grandparents, and even making them up in my head. Thankfully, I had a FANTASTIC librarian in elementary school. She was a phenomenal storyteller, especially during Halloween. The stories I heard and the books I read were able to take me to a different world. I am thankful that she instilled sucha a love of reading for me and I hope to do the same one day.

Goals as a Librarian

  1. Help students find books they're interested in
  2. Encourage reading outside of comfort zone
  3. Collaborate with other faculty
  4. Be a resource to ALL students and staff in the building
